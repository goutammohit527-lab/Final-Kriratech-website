Entry Exit Counter Display

Automated People Counting System with Real-Time Display

Product Overview

The Entry Exit Counter Display is an automated people tracking system that accurately counts individuals entering and exiting a premises in real-time. The system uses sensor-based detection with a prominent digital display showing current occupancy levels.

Ideal for retail stores, malls, offices, event venues, and public spaces for occupancy management, crowd control, and compliance with capacity regulations.
