VOC Tracker

Volatile Organic Compound Detection & Tracking System

Product Overview

The VOC Tracker is a specialized monitoring system designed to detect and track Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) in indoor air environments. VOCs are harmful chemical compounds emitted from paints, cleaning products, furniture, and industrial processes that can affect health and air quality.

This advanced device continuously monitors VOC levels and provides real-time data through IoT connectivity, enabling proactive air quality management. Perfect for industrial facilities, laboratories, offices, and residential spaces where VOC monitoring is critical for health and safety.

Key Features

  • High-precision VOC detection (ppb level accuracy)
  • Real-time monitoring with trend analysis
  • Cloud-based data logging and historical tracking
  • Automatic alerts when VOC levels exceed safe thresholds
  • Multi-device dashboard for comprehensive monitoring
  • Integration with ventilation and air purification systems
  • Mobile app support for remote monitoring

Applications

Paint manufacturing and automotive industries
Chemical laboratories and research facilities
Furniture manufacturing and woodworking shops
Offices and commercial buildings
Pharmaceutical production facilities
Indoor air quality assessment in homes
